Library

The library can be found directly opposite the Administration block. There are two ways to get to the library from the administration block. Once out of the administration block, you can take a right and walk straiht. You will come across a walk way on your left. Walk up the walk way till you come to the Lecture Hall 217. Take a left and walk straight. You will find the Library a few meters away from that lecture hall.

Alternative Route: Once out of the administration block, you can take a left turn and walk till you come across the rest room. Take a right turn, walk till you come to the essential store. Take the stairs and go up. When you get to the next floor, take a left turn. You will find the Library in front of you.

Engineering Block

The Engineering Block can be found behind the Library. When you enter the school from the main entrance beside the Administration Block, take the walk way you find in front of you. At the end of the walk way, take a left and continue forward. Next to the Library, take a right. You will find the Engineering Block in front of you.
